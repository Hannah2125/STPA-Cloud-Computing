Privatization of Space Exploration


Space Exploration Technologies and SpaceX operated the Dragon spacecraft. It carried cargo to the International Space Station, the ISS, while following the agreements that SpaceX has with NASA. The Dragon spacecraft is the first private spacecraft to berth with the International Sapce Station.
The Arsani X Prize is was a challenge to create a resuable, privately funded, reliable, manned spacecraft that can carry three people one hundred kilometers above the Earth twice in two weeks.The prize for completing the challenge was ten million dollars. On June 21st,2004, the SpaceShipOne, a manned spacecraft, flew sixty-two and a half miles above Earth's atmosphere. Which was the first non-government manned spacecraft to do so. On September 29th and October 4th,2004 the spacecraft flew again. The SpaceShipOne ended up winning the Arsani X Prize for being the first privately developed, reliable, reusable spacecraft.
Team Moon Express is one of the two teams in the Google Lunat XPRIZE that have verified launch contract for their 2017 lunar mission. Moon Express is the first company the U.S government has granted approval to launch contract to launch to the moon. The moon express lunar mission is set for 2017. A meeting was held between the Federal Aviation Administration, The White House, and the state Department and Moon Express was given approval to launch.
